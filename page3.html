Threat Model Manifesto of ABC Restaurant Chain

1. What are we working on?

Business Requirements

  • Make sure the restaurant chain can serve customers during operating hours, both in-person and online.
  • Protect customer data, including personal information and payment details.
  • Securely process payments to prevent fraud.
  • Maintain a good reputation to keep customers coming back.
  • Ensure that menu items and prices are accurate and consistent across all locations.

Technical Requirements

  • The restaurant chain relies on POS systems to process orders and handle payments.
  • The online and mobile apps for ordering food must be secure and easy to use.
  • Customer information and order history must be stored securely in a database.
  • A reliable and secure network is needed for communication between restaurant locations and data centers.
  • The restaurant chain must integrate with secure payment gateways for processing online transactions.
  • Employee access to sensitive systems and data must be restricted using role-based access control.
  • Secure communication and data sharing must be in place with suppliers and vendors.
  • A plan for responding to security incidents must be developed to minimize potential damage.

The ABC Restaurant Chain is working on securing the entire restaurant operation, including customer data, payment processing, and supply chain management.

Key Assets

  • Customer data: This includes personal information such as names, addresses, and payment details. It is critical to protect this data, as a breach could result in reputation damage and legal consequences.
  • Payment systems: These systems are used to process payments from customers. It is important to ensure the security of these systems to prevent financial fraud.
  • Supply chain management: This includes the process of ensuring that food is safe and of high quality. It is important to manage the security of the supply chain to prevent tampering or contamination of food.
  • Inventory and supplies: These include ingredients, food items, and kitchen supplies. It is important to protect these assets to ensure the smooth operation of the restaurant.
  • Brand reputation: The ABC Restaurant Chain's reputation is a valuable asset. Any security incident, such as a data breach or food safety issue, can damage this reputation, impacting business operations and customer trust.

Prioritization

  • Customer data: This is the highest priority asset, as a breach could have a significant impact on the restaurant's reputation and financial well-being.
  • Payment systems: These systems are also a high priority, as a breach could result in financial losses.
  • Supply chain management: This is a lower priority asset, but it is still important to ensure the security of the supply chain to prevent food safety issues.
  • Inventory and supplies: These assets are also important to protect, but they are not as critical as customer data or payment systems.
  • Brand reputation: This is a valuable asset, but it is not as critical as customer data or payment systems.

Security plays a significant role in supporting the restaurant business in several ways:

  • Customer trust: Robust security measures, especially for customer data protection, build trust. When customers trust that their information is safe, they are more likely to dine in or order from the restaurant.
  • Legal compliance: Security measures ensure compliance with data protection regulations, avoiding costly fines and legal issues.
  • Financial protection: Securing financial assets and payment systems protects the restaurant from financial fraud and loss.
  • Food safety: Security in the form of supply chain integrity and kitchen safety measures ensures the quality and safety of food, which is essential for customer health and satisfaction.
  • Operational continuity: Security measures protect against disruptions, whether due to cyberattacks or physical incidents, ensuring smooth restaurant operations.
  • Brand reputation: By safeguarding customer data, food quality, and overall safety, security supports and enhances the restaurant's brand reputation.

The ABC Restaurant Chain is committed to protecting its assets and ensuring the security of its operations. By implementing appropriate security measures, the restaurant can mitigate the risks of a data breach or other security incident and protect its reputation and bottom line.
